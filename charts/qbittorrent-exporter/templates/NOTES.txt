1. Get the exporter service:
   kubectl get svc {{ include "qbittorrent-exporter.fullname" . }} -n {{ .Release.Namespace }}

2. Check the metrics endpoint:
   kubectl port-forward svc/{{ include "qbittorrent-exporter.fullname" . }} 8090:{{ .Values.service.port }} -n {{ .Release.Namespace }}

3. Visit:
   http://127.0.0.1:8090/metrics
